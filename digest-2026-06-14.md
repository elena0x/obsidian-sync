### Latent Space: Mistral — Voxtral TTS, Forge, Leanstral, & What's Next

**English:** The Latent Space podcast featured Mistral's Chief Scientist Guillaume Lample and audio research lead Pavan Kumar Reddy discussing Mistral's new Voxtral TTS (text-to-speech) model. Key takeaways: Voxtral TTS is a 3.6B parameter model supporting 9 languages, built on a novel autoregressive flow matching architecture with an in-house neural audio codec. It converts audio into 12.5 Hz latent tokens (semantic + acoustic) and uses flow matching instead of discrete token prediction for better quality and efficiency. The model is designed for real-time voice agent applications, with plans to eventually build a full duplex (audio-in-audio-out) model. Mistral also discussed their "Forge" platform for enterprise model customization and fine-tuning, emphasizing that fine-tuning on proprietary data can create 10x cost advantages over closed-source models. They also teased Leanstral — a model for formal mathematical proving using Lean, which enables verifiable reasoning without the reward hacking issues that plague traditional RL approaches.

**中文：** Latent Space 播客邀请了 Mistral 首席科学家 Guillaume Lample 和音频研究负责人 Pavan Kumar Reddy，讨论 Mistral 新的 Voxtral TTS（文本转语音）模型。关键要点：Voxtral TTS 是一个 36 亿参数的模型，支持 9 种语言，基于新颖的自回归流匹配架构和内部神经音频编解码器。它将音频转换为 12.5 Hz 的潜在 token（语义 + 声学），并使用流匹配而非离散 token 预测来获得更好的质量和效率。该模型专为实时语音 agent 应用设计，计划最终构建全双工（音频输入/输出）模型。Mistral 还讨论了他们的 "Forge" 企业模型定制和微调平台，强调在专有数据上微调可以比闭源模型创造 10 倍的成本优势。他们还预告了 Leanstral——一个使用 Lean 进行形式数学证明的模型，可实现可验证的推理，而不会出现困扰传统 RL 方法的奖励黑客问题。
