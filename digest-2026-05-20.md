### Aaron Levie (Box CEO)

Levie argues that AI agents don't eliminate work — they move it up a layer of abstraction. The new work becomes: figuring out what to tell the agent, giving proper instructions, providing context, intervening when it goes off-task, reviewing output, and incorporating it into broader work. As agents handle longer-horizon tasks, planning and review stages expand. You become the editor, manager, producer. Taste, craft understanding, and skills still matter. Agents automate the annoying parts and keep the enjoyable parts — but the work is still there, just different.

Box CEO Aaron Levie 认为 AI agent 并没有消除工作，而是将工作提升了一层抽象。新的工作变成了：想清楚要让 agent 做什么、给出正确指令、提供上下文、在 agent 偏离时干预、审核输出、将其整合到更大的工作中。随着 agent 处理更长期的任务，规划和审核阶段反而会扩展。你变成了编辑、经理、制片人。品味、对工艺的理解和技能仍然重要。Agent 自动化了烦人的部分，保留了有趣的部分——但工作还在，只是形式不同。

### Latent Space: Mistral — Voxtral TTS, Forge, Leanstral, & what's next for Mistral 4

**The Takeaway:** Mistral is betting on a modular, capability-specific model strategy rather than one giant omni-model — releasing specialized audio, vision, and coding models that eventually merge into sparse generalist models like Mistral Small (6B active, 256K context).

核心要点：Mistral 押注于模块化、能力特定的模型策略，而非一个巨大的全能模型——发布专门的音频、视觉和编码模型，最终合并为稀疏通用模型如 Mistral Small（6B 活跃参数，256K 上下文）。

Mistral Chief Scientist Guillaume Lample and Audio Research Lead Pavan Kumar Reddy joined Latent Space to discuss three major releases: Voxtral TTS (their first speech generation model), Forge (their enterprise fine-tuning platform), and Leanstral (their first formal math/reasoning model).

Mistral 首席科学家 Guillaume Lample 和音频研究负责人 Pavan Kumar Reddy 做客 Latent Space，讨论了三大发布：Voxtral TTS（他们的首个语音生成模型）、Forge（企业微调平台）和 Leanstral（首个形式化数学/推理模型）。

**Voxtral TTS** is a 3B model supporting 9 languages, built on their Ministral backbone. What makes it novel is the architecture: instead of the common "depth transformer" approach (which requires K autoregressive steps and adds latency), they developed an autoregressive flow matching architecture with an in-house neural audio codec that converts audio into 12.5 Hertz latent tokens (semantic + acoustic). This allows inference in just 4-16 steps instead of K sequential steps — a significant latency win for real-time voice agents.

**Voxtral TTS** 是一个支持 9 种语言的 3B 模型，基于 Ministral 骨干网络。其新颖之处在于架构：他们没有采用常见的"深度 transformer"方法（需要 K 个自回归步骤，增加延迟），而是开发了自回归流匹配架构，配合内部神经音频编解码器，将音频转换为 12.5 Hertz 的潜在 token（语义+声学）。这使得推理只需 4-16 步，而不是 K 个顺序步骤——对实时语音 agent 来说是显著的延迟优势。

**On audio vs text modeling:** Pavan explained that unlike text where a word typically maps to a single token, "even the same word could be inflected in so many different ways" in audio. Flow matching works better because it models the distribution of possible audio realizations rather than trying to predict a single mean (which would produce "blurred out speech"). This is why they chose flow matching over discrete diffusion — it handles the inherent entropy of speech more naturally.

**关于音频与文本建模：** Pavan 解释说，与文本中一个单词通常对应单个 token 不同，"在音频中，即使是同一个单词也可以用如此多不同的方式发音"。流匹配效果更好，因为它建模了可能的音频实现的分布，而不是试图预测单一均值（这会产生"模糊的语音"）。这就是他们选择流匹配而非离散扩散的原因——它更自然地处理语音固有的熵。

**On enterprise strategy:** Guillaume emphasized that Mistral's enterprise pitch is not just "here's an API" but deep customer collaboration. They deploy models on-premise for privacy-sensitive data, help customers fine-tune on their proprietary data ("trillions of tokens in very specific domains"), and build customized solutions — like a 3B audio model with function calling for an automotive client that needed offline capability. He claimed they can often build solutions "10x cheaper" than closed-source alternatives through targeted fine-tuning.

**关于企业战略：** Guillaume 强调，Mistral 的企业策略不只是"给你一个 API"，而是深度客户合作。他们为隐私敏感数据在本地部署模型，帮助客户在专有数据上微调（"非常特定领域的数万亿 token"），并构建定制解决方案——比如为一个需要离线能力的汽车客户构建了一个带函数调用的 3B 音频模型。他声称通过有针对性的微调，他们往往能以闭源替代方案"十分之一的成本"构建解决方案。

**On open source:** Guillaume framed open-weight releases as core to Mistral's DNA — "we really don't want to be living in a world where the smartest model, the best models are only behind closed doors." They release detailed technical reports (for Magistral, their reasoning model, and the real-time audio model) to help the science community move faster.

**关于开源：** Guillaume 将开放权重发布定位为 Mistral 的 DNA 核心——"我们真的不想生活在一个最聪明、最好的模型只关在门后的世界"。他们发布详细的技术报告（针对 Magistral、他们的推理模型和实时音频模型）以帮助科学社区更快进步。

**On Leanstral:** Guillaume explained their formal math model — reasoning without RL is hard because most real-world problems lack easy verification (unlike AIME problems where you can compare to a reference answer). Lean provides automatic verification: "if it compiles in Lean, it's correct." They see applications beyond math to software verification, an industry currently "tiny" but poised to grow as coding agents make formal verification more accessible.

**关于 Leanstral：** Guillaume 解释了他们的形式化数学模型——没有 RL 的推理很难，因为大多数现实世界的问题缺乏简单的验证（不像 AIME 问题可以与参考答案比较）。Lean 提供自动验证："如果它在 Lean 中编译通过，就是正确的"。他们看到了超越数学的应用，延伸到软件验证——一个目前"很小"但 poised to grow 的行业，因为 coding agent 使形式化验证更易获得。
