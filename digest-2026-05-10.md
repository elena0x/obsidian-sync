### Aaron Levie (Box CEO)

Aaron Levie 深入阐述了 AI agents 并未消除工作，而是将工作提升了一层抽象。当 agents 为你执行任务时，你的工作变成了：明确指令、提供上下文、监控偏差、审查成果、整合输出。任何一个环节出错，产出就可能毫无价值。随着任务时间跨度变长，规划和审查阶段反而会更加重要。你变成了编辑、管理者、制片人——品味、对 craft 的理解和技能依然至关重要。

Aaron Levie argues that AI agents haven't eliminated work — they've moved it up a layer of abstraction. When agents do work for you, your job becomes: figuring out what to tell the agent, giving proper instructions, providing context, intervening when it goes off-task, reviewing the final output, and incorporating it into something else. Any one of these being off, and you get useless work product. As agents handle longer-horizon tasks, planning and review stages take up even more time. You become the editor, the manager, the producer — taste, understanding of the craft, and skills still matter deeply.

### Latent Space: Mistral — Voxtral TTS, Forge, Leansral, & What's Next for Mistral 4

**The Takeaway:** Mistral 正在以"分而治之，再统一"的策略构建下一代 AI 能力——先由各团队独立优化特定能力（语音、视觉、代码、推理），再合并为统一的稀疏 MoE 模型，同时坚持开放权重和详细技术报告的开源精神。

Mistral is building next-gen AI capabilities with a "divide and conquer, then unify" strategy — individual teams optimize specific capabilities (voice, vision, code, reasoning) before merging them into unified sparse MoE models, while maintaining their open-weights ethos and detailed technical reports.

Guillaume Lample（首席科学家）和 Pavan Kumar Reddy（音频研究负责人）做客 Latent Space，深入讨论了 Mistral 的最新发布。Voxtral TTS 是 Mistral 首个语音生成模型，基于 3B 参数的 Ministral 主干，支持 9 种语言，采用创新的自回归流匹配（autoregressive flow matching）架构和自研神经音频编解码器，将音频转换为 12.5Hz 的语义+声学 latent tokens。相比竞品，Voxtral TTS 在同等质量下成本仅为一小部分，且已开放权重。

Guillaume Lample (Chief Scientist) and Pavan Kumar Reddy (Head of Audio Research) join Latent Space to discuss Mistral's latest releases. Voxtral TTS is Mistral's first speech generation model, built on a 3B-parameter Ministral backbone, supporting 9 languages, using a novel autoregressive flow matching architecture and in-house neural audio codec that converts audio into 12.5Hz semantic + acoustic latent tokens. Compared to competitors, Voxtral TTS costs a fraction while maintaining comparable quality, and the weights are open.

Pavan 解释了为何选择流匹配而非传统的深度 transformer：音频在每个时间步都有多种可能的表达方式（同一个人说同一个词可以有无数种语调），流匹配能更好地建模这种分布，且推理仅需 4-16 步，latency 大幅降低。他们的终极目标是构建全双工（full duplex）模型——能边听边说——但目前采取分步策略：先优化 transcription，再 speech generation，再实时能力，最后统一。

Pavan explains why they chose flow matching over traditional depth transformers: at each timestep, audio has many possible expressions (the same word in your voice can be inflected infinitely many ways), and flow matching models this distribution better. Inference takes only 4-16 steps, cutting latency significantly. Their ultimate goal is a full-duplex model that can speak while listening, but they're taking it step by step: transcription first, then speech generation, then real-time capabilities, then unification.

Guillaume 还谈到了 Mistral 的更大愿景：开源是公司的 DNA，他们不想生活在一个最聪明的模型被锁在几家公司门后的世界。他们发布了 Leanstral——一个形式化数学证明模型，因为 Lean 编译通过即证明正确，解决了传统推理中"无法验证证明正确性"的难题。同时，Mistral 4（即 Mistral Small）是一个 6B 激活参数的稀疏 MoE 模型，256K 上下文，首次将代码（Devstral）、视觉（Pixtral）和文本能力统一到一个模型中。

Guillaume also discusses Mistral's broader vision: open source is in the company's DNA, and they don't want to live in a world where the smartest models are locked behind closed doors. They released Leanstral — a formal mathematical proof model — because in Lean, "if it compiles, it's correct," solving the problem of verifying proof correctness that plagues traditional reasoning. Meanwhile, Mistral 4 (aka Mistral Small) is a 6B-active-parameter sparse MoE with 256K context, unifying code (Devstral), vision (Pixtral), and text capabilities for the first time.

> — Guillaume Lample, Mistral Chief Scientist
