### Box CEO Aaron Levie

Aaron Levie offered a nuanced take on AI agents: they don't eliminate work, they shift it up a layer of abstraction. The new work becomes planning what to tell the agent, providing proper instructions and context, intervening when it goes off-task, reviewing output, and incorporating it into broader work. "You are now the editor. A manager. A producer. The taste matters, understanding the craft matters, and the skills ultimately matter." He argues AI will automate the annoying parts while keeping the more enjoyable, creative aspects.

Box CEO Aaron Levie 对 AI Agent 提出了 nuanced 的观点：它们不会消除工作，而是将工作提升到更高的抽象层次。新的工作变成了规划如何向 Agent 下达指令、提供适当的指令和上下文、在 Agent 偏离任务时进行干预、审查输出并将其整合到更广泛的工作中。"你现在成了编辑、经理、制作人。品味很重要，理解工艺很重要，技能最终也很重要。"他认为 AI 将自动化工作中烦人的部分，同时保留更有趣、更具创造性的方面。

### Latent Space: Mistral — Voxtral TTS, Forge, Leanstral, & what's next for Mistral 4

**The Takeaway:** Mistral is methodically building toward a full-duplex voice agent by releasing specialized, efficient audio models first — starting with transcription, now adding text-to-speech — rather than jumping straight to an omni model.

**核心要点：** Mistral 正通过先发布专门的、高效的音频模型——从转录开始，现在加入文本转语音——逐步构建全双工语音 Agent，而不是直接跳到全能模型。

Mistral Chief Scientist Guillaume Lample and Audio Research Lead Pavan Kumar Reddy joined Latent Space to discuss three major releases: Voxtral TTS (their first speech generation model), Mistral Small (a merged multi-capability model), and Leanstral (a formal math proving model).

Mistral 首席科学家 Guillaume Lample 和音频研究负责人 Pavan Kumar Reddy 做客 Latent Space，讨论了三大发布：Voxtral TTS（他们的首个语音生成模型）、Mistral Small（一个合并了多种能力的模型）和 Leanstral（一个形式化数学证明模型）。

**On Voxtral TTS:** The model uses a novel autoregressive flow matching architecture with an in-house neural audio codec that converts audio into 12.5 Hz latent tokens (semantic + acoustic). At just 3B parameters, it achieves quality comparable to top competitors at a fraction of the cost. Pavan noted that flow matching outperformed discrete diffusion approaches because audio has more entropy than text — "even the same word with your own voice could be inflected in so many different ways." The model supports 9 languages and is designed for real-time streaming voice agents.

**关于 Voxtral TTS：** 该模型使用新颖的自回归流匹配架构，配合内部神经音频编解码器，将音频转换为 12.5 Hz 的潜在 token（语义+声学）。仅 30 亿参数，就能达到与顶级竞争对手相当的质量，但成本仅为一小部分。Pavan 指出，流匹配优于离散扩散方法，因为音频比文本具有更高的熵——"即使是你自己的声音，同一个词也可以用许多不同的方式发音。"该模型支持 9 种语言，专为实时流式语音 Agent 设计。

**On Mistral Small:** This is Mistral's first merged model combining general instruction following (Devstral for coding, Magistral for math, Pixtral for vision) into a single 6B active parameter sparse model with 256K context. Guillaume explained their philosophy: build specialized models for each capability, then merge once mature — rather than starting with one giant generalist.

**关于 Mistral Small：** 这是 Mistral 首个合并模型，将通用指令遵循（Devstral 用于编程、Magistral 用于数学、Pixtral 用于视觉）整合到一个仅 60 亿活跃参数的稀疏模型中，上下文长度达 256K。Guillaume 解释了他们的理念：为每种能力构建专门的模型，成熟后再合并——而不是从一个巨大的通才模型开始。

**On Leanstral:** Guillaume described formal math proving as the next frontier for reasoning. Unlike math competition problems where answers are verifiable (AIME-style), most real mathematical reasoning involves proofs with no easy verification. Lean (a formal proof language) solves this — "if it compiles in Lean, it's correct." Mistral sees applications beyond math to software verification, an industry currently limited to safety-critical domains like aerospace but poised to expand dramatically with coding agents.

**关于 Leanstral：** Guillaume 将形式化数学证明描述为推理的下一个前沿。与答案可验证的数学竞赛问题（AIME 风格）不同，大多数真正的数学推理涉及难以验证的证明。Lean（一种形式化证明语言）解决了这个问题——"如果它在 Lean 中编译通过，它就是正确的。"Mistral 看到了除数学之外的软件验证应用，这个行业目前仅限于航空航天等安全关键领域，但随着编程 Agent 的发展，有望大幅扩展。

**On open source:** Both emphasized that open sourcing is "in the DNA of the company." Guillaume traced this back to his and Tim's work on LLaMA at Meta, which democratized LLM research. They want to avoid a future where "the smartest models are only behind closed doors."

**关于开源：** 两人都强调开源"是公司的 DNA"。Guillaume 追溯到他和 Tim 在 Meta 的 LLaMA 工作，这项工作使 LLM 研究民主化。他们希望避免"最聪明的模型只被关在封闭门后"的未来。

**On enterprise deployment:** Guillaume made a strong case for on-premise fine-tuning. Companies have collected trillions of domain-specific tokens over years that closed-source models never see. Fine-tuning on this proprietary data creates a "very big advantage" — the model knows the company's entire knowledge base without needing 10K tokens of context per query. Mistral's Forge platform provides the same battle-tested infrastructure their science team uses.

**关于企业部署：** Guillaume 为本地微调提出了有力的论据。公司多年来收集了数万亿领域特定的 token，闭源模型从未见过这些数据。在这些专有数据上微调创造了"非常大的优势"——模型了解公司的整个知识库，每次查询不需要 10K token 的上下文。Mistral 的 Forge 平台提供与他们科学团队使用的相同经过实战检验的基础设施。

**Key quote from Guillaume:** "Using your closed source model is really sad because it basically puts you in a position where you are not leveraging all this data, and you are going to be using the same model as all your competitors when you could actually use everything you have been collecting for years, which is really valuable."

**Guillaume 的关键引述：** "使用闭源模型真的很令人遗憾，因为这让你处于无法利用所有这些数据的位置，你将和所有竞争对手使用相同的模型，而你实际上可以使用多年来收集的一切，这些真的很有价值。"
