Aaron Levie (CEO @ Box)

Aaron Levie argues that AI agents haven't eliminated work — they've moved it up a layer of abstraction. The new work is: figuring out what to tell the agent, giving proper instructions, providing context, intervening when it goes off-task, reviewing output, and incorporating it into the final product. As agents handle longer-horizon tasks, planning and review stages actually expand. "You are now the editor. A manager. A producer. The taste matters, understanding the craft matters, and the skills ultimately matter." https://x.com/levie/status/2041347596342460439
Aaron Levie 认为 AI Agent 并没有消除工作，而是将工作提升了一层抽象。新的工作包括：告诉 Agent 做什么、给出正确指令、提供上下文、在 Agent 偏离时干预、审核输出并将其融入最终产品。随着 Agent 处理更长期的任务，规划和审核阶段实际上会扩大。"你现在是一名编辑、一位经理、一个制作人。品味很重要，对手艺的理解很重要，技能最终也很重要。" https://x.com/levie/status/2041347596342460439
