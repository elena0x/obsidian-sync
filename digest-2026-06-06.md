### Peter Yang (Product at Roblox)

Peter Yang shared five key takeaways from a conversation with a16z GP Anish Acharya about the future of work in an AI agent-first world. He argues that coding will eat all knowledge work — he now gets the first 80% of docs, slides, and analytics done through AI coding agents before manual polish. Small teams will outperform large orgs: 2-3 person product teams with agent swarms will replace overstaffed companies. Apps for completing tasks will shrink as agents handle the work; entertainment apps will outlast productivity ones. He also highlighted the emergence of deeply personal agents — his OpenClaw once reminded him to prioritize his kids over career. Finally, he notes that human ambition has no ceiling — the economy is changing shape, not shrinking, with more one-person companies emerging.

Peter Yang 与 a16z GP Anish Acharya 讨论了 AI agent 时代的工作未来，分享了五个关键观点。他认为编程将吞噬所有知识工作——他现在通过 AI coding agent 完成文档、幻灯片和分析的 80% 初稿，再手动打磨。小团队将击败大组织：2-3 人的产品团队配合 agent swarm 将取代人员臃肿的公司。完成任务类的应用会萎缩，因为 agent 会处理这些工作；娱乐类应用将比生产力工具更持久。他还提到深度个人化 agent 的兴起——他的 OpenClaw 曾提醒他多陪孩子而非只顾事业。最后他指出人类野心没有上限——经济在变形而非萎缩，将出现更多一人公司。

### Aaron Levie (Box CEO)

Aaron Levie argues that AI agents haven't eliminated work — they've moved it up a layer of abstraction. The new work is figuring out what to tell the agent, giving proper instructions, providing context, intervening when it goes off-task, reviewing output, and incorporating it into something else. The longer the task, the more planning and review stages take up. You become the editor, manager, producer — taste matters, understanding the craft matters, and skills ultimately matter. AI automates the annoying parts but keeps the enjoyable parts; the work is still there, just different.

Aaron Levie 认为 AI agent 并没有消除工作，而是将工作提升了一层抽象。新的工作变成了：告诉 agent 做什么、给出正确指令、提供上下文、在偏离时干预、审查产出、整合到其他地方。任务越长，规划和审查阶段占比越大。你变成了编辑、经理、制作人——品味重要，理解手艺重要，技能最终仍然重要。AI 自动化了烦人的部分，保留了有趣的部分；工作还在，只是形式变了。

### Latent Space: Mistral — Voxtral TTS, Forge, Leanstral, & what's next for Mistral 4

**The Takeaway:** Mistral is betting on specialized, efficient models over giant generalists — their new Voxtral TTS is a 3B parameter model that matches the best competitors at a fraction of the cost, and they're building toward full-duplex voice agents that can speak while you speak.

**核心要点：** Mistral 押注于专业化、高效的模型而非巨型通用模型——他们新的 Voxtral TTS 是一个 30 亿参数的模型，以极低的成本达到了最佳竞争对手的水平，他们正在构建能够在你说话时同时说话的全双工语音 agent。

Mistral Chief Scientist Guillaume Lample and Audio Research Lead Pavan Kumar Reddy joined Latent Space to discuss their latest releases. Voxtral TTS is their first speech generation model, supporting 9 languages and built on a novel autoregressive flow matching architecture with an in-house neural audio codec. The codec converts audio into 12.5 Hz latent tokens with both semantic and acoustic components. Rather than using a depth transformer to predict multiple tokens autoregressively (which adds latency), they use a flow matching head that can generate in as few as 4-16 steps, with potential to reach single-step inference.

Mistral 首席科学家 Guillaume Lample 和音频研究负责人 Pavan Kumar Reddy 加入 Latent Space 讨论他们的最新发布。Voxtral TTS 是他们的首个语音生成模型，支持 9 种语言，基于新颖的自回归流匹配架构和自研神经音频编解码器构建。该编解码器将音频转换为 12.5 Hz 的潜在 token，包含语义和声学组件。他们没有使用深度 Transformer 来自回归地预测多个 token（这会增加延迟），而是使用流匹配头，可以在仅 4-16 步内生成，并有潜力达到单步推理。

Pavan explained that unlike text where each word maps to a single token, audio has far more entropy — the same word can be inflected countless ways. Flow matching models this distribution better than discrete approaches. The model is built on their 3B Minstral backbone and designed for real-time streaming voice agents, which is their primary use case.

Pavan 解释说，与文本中每个单词映射到单个 token 不同，音频具有更高的熵——同一个单词可以有无数种语调变化。流匹配比离散方法更好地建模这种分布。该模型基于他们的 3B Minstral 骨干网络构建，专为实时流式语音 agent 设计，这是他们的主要用例。

Guillaume emphasized Mistral's strategy of building specialized, efficient models rather than one giant generalist. They also discussed Forge, their enterprise platform for fine-tuning and deployment, and Leanstral — their formal math proving model. On open source, Guillaume was unequivocal: "We really don't want to be living in a world where the smartest models are only behind closed doors... We want intelligence to be accessible to anyone."

Guillaume 强调了 Mistral 构建专业化、高效模型而非一个巨型通用模型的策略。他们还讨论了 Forge——他们的企业微调与部署平台，以及 Leanstral——他们的形式化数学证明模型。在开源方面，Guillaume 态度明确："我们真的不想生活在一个最智能的模型只关在门后的世界里……我们希望智能对任何人都是可访问的。"
