**Latent Space: Mistral's Voxtral TTS, Forge, Leanstral & What's Next**

Mistral Chief Scientist Guillaume Lample and Audio Research Lead Pavan Kumar Reddy join Latent Space to discuss Mistral's latest releases. The big announcement: **Voxtral TTS**, their first speech generation model, built on a novel autoregressive flow matching architecture with an in-house neural audio codec. It's a 3B parameter model that runs at a fraction of competitor costs while matching top-tier quality, supporting 9 languages with open weights.

The most striking insight: flow matching in audio is still novel territory. Unlike text where one word maps to one token, the same word in audio can be inflected infinitely many ways. Flow matching models this distribution naturally, avoiding the "blurred out speech" problem of predicting means. Pavan notes the audio field hasn't converged on a "winner model" yet — unlike vision or text, the best architecture is still being discovered.

On the business side, Guillaume emphasizes Mistral's strategy of specialized, efficient models over one giant generalist. A 1B or 3B audio model performs transcription just as well as a massive general model at a fraction of the cost. They also discuss **Leanstral** — their formal math proving model — as a bet on verifiable reasoning. In formal systems like Lean, "if it compiles, it's correct," solving the reward hacking problem that plagues LLM judges for open-ended proofs.

The episode also covers Mistral's enterprise playbook: helping customers fine-tune on their proprietary data (sometimes trillions of domain-specific tokens) and deploy on-premise for privacy. "Using closed source models is sad because it puts you at the same level as all your competitors."

**Latent Space：Mistral 的 Voxtral TTS、Forge、Leanstral 及未来方向**

Mistral 首席科学家 Guillaume Lample 和音频研究负责人 Pavan Kumar Reddy 做客 Latent Space，讨论 Mistral 的最新发布。核心 announcement：**Voxtral TTS**，他们首个语音生成模型，基于新颖的自回归流匹配架构和自研神经音频编解码器。这是一个 30 亿参数的模型，以竞争对手零头的成本运行，同时达到顶级质量，支持 9 种语言并开放权重。

最引人注目的洞察：流匹配在音频领域仍是新颖 territory。与文本中一个词对应一个 token 不同，同一个词在音频中可以有无限多种语调变化。流匹配自然地建模了这一分布，避免了预测均值导致的"模糊语音"问题。Pavan 指出音频领域尚未收敛到"赢家模型"——与视觉或文本不同，最佳架构仍在探索中。

在商业层面，Guillaume 强调 Mistral 的策略是专用、高效的模型，而非一个巨型通用模型。10 亿或 30 亿的音频模型在转录任务上的表现与庞大的通用模型一样好，但成本只是零头。他们还讨论了 **Leanstral**——他们的形式化数学证明模型——作为对可验证推理的押注。在 Lean 等形式化系统中，"如果能编译，就是正确的"，解决了开放式证明中困扰 LLM 评判器的奖励黑客问题。

本集还涵盖了 Mistral 的企业 playbook：帮助客户在其专有数据（有时数万亿领域特定 token）上进行微调，并支持本地部署以保护隐私。"使用闭源模型是可悲的，因为它让你和所有竞争对手处于同一水平。"
