### Aaron Levie (CEO @ Box)

Aaron Levie argues that AI agents haven't eliminated work—they've moved it up a layer of abstraction. The new work is figuring out what to tell the agent, giving proper instructions, providing context, intervening when it goes off-task, reviewing output, and incorporating it into the final product. "You are now the editor. A manager. A producer." The longer the task, the more planning and review stages matter. Taste, craft understanding, and skills still matter—agents just automate the annoying parts.

Aaron Levie 认为 AI Agent 并没有消除工作，而是将工作提升了一个抽象层级。新的工作变成了：告诉 Agent 做什么、给出正确指令、提供上下文、在 Agent 偏离任务时干预、审核最终产出、并将其整合到其他工作中。"你现在成了编辑、经理、制片人。"任务越长，规划和审核阶段越重要。品味、对手艺的理解和技能依然关键——Agent 只是自动化了烦人的部分。

### Latent Space: Mistral — Voxtral TTS, Forge, Leanstral, & what's next for Mistral 4

**The Takeaway:** Mistral is betting that specialized, efficient models beat generalist giants—starting with a 3B-parameter TTS model that matches top competitors at a fraction of the cost.

Mistral 首席科学家 Guillaume Lample 和音频研究负责人 Pavan Kumar Reddy 做客 Latent Space，发布了 Voxtral TTS——Mistral 首个语音生成模型。基于 3B 参数的 Ministral 骨干网络，支持 9 种语言，以极低成本达到顶尖质量。技术亮点包括：自研神经音频编解码器将音频转为 12.5Hz 的语义+声学 token；首创在音频领域应用流匹配（flow matching）架构替代传统的深度自回归 transformer，大幅降低推理延迟；开源权重发布。

Mistral's chief scientist Guillaume Lample and audio research lead Pavan Kumar Reddy joined Latent Space to launch Voxtral TTS—Mistral's first speech generation model. Built on a 3B-parameter Ministral backbone, it supports 9 languages and matches top competitors at a fraction of the cost. Technical highlights: a novel neural audio codec converts audio into 12.5Hz semantic + acoustic tokens; pioneering use of flow matching architecture in audio generation (replacing traditional deep autoregressive transformers), dramatically reducing inference latency; open weights released.

Guillaume emphasized Mistral's strategy of efficient, specialized models over expensive generalists—deployable on-premise for privacy-sensitive enterprises, with fine-tuning on proprietary data yielding 10x better results than closed-source alternatives. The roadmap points toward full-duplex voice agents and eventually a unified "super omni model."

Guillaume 强调了 Mistral 专注于高效专用模型而非昂贵通用模型的战略——可本地部署满足隐私敏感企业需求，在专有数据上微调的效果比闭源替代方案好 10 倍。路线图指向全双工语音 Agent，最终目标是统一的"超级全能模型"。
