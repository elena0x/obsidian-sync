### Latent Space: Mistral — Voxtral TTS, Forge, Leanstral, & what's next for Mistral 4

**The Takeaway:** Mistral is betting that audio generation is the next frontier, and they're building it with a novel flow-matching architecture that makes a 3B parameter model competitive with much larger rivals at a fraction of the cost.

**核心要点：** Mistral 押注音频生成是下一个前沿领域，他们正在用新颖的 flow-matching 架构构建它，使一个 30 亿参数的模型能够以极低的成本与更大的竞争对手抗衡。

Mistral Chief Scientist Guillaume Lample and Head of Audio Research Pavan Kumar Reddy joined Latent Space to discuss their latest releases. The headline is Voxtral TTS — Mistral's first speech generation model, though not their first audio model (they previously released transcription models with real-time capabilities).

Mistral 首席科学家 Guillaume Lample 和音频研究负责人 Pavan Kumar Reddy 做客 Latent Space，讨论了他们最新的发布。重头戏是 Voxtral TTS——Mistral 的首个语音生成模型，虽然不是他们的首个音频模型（他们之前发布过具备实时能力的转录模型）。

The model supports nine languages and is remarkably small at just 3B parameters, built on top of their Ministral model. What makes it technically interesting is the architecture: instead of the common depth transformer approach for predicting multiple audio tokens per timestep, Mistral developed a novel autoregressive flow-matching head combined with an in-house neural audio codec. This codec converts audio into 12.5 Hz latent tokens with both semantic and acoustic components.

该模型支持九种语言，参数仅 30 亿，基于他们的 Ministral 模型构建。技术上最有趣的是其架构：Mistral 没有采用常见的 depth transformer 方法来预测每个时间步的多个音频 token，而是开发了一种新颖的自回归 flow-matching head，结合自研的神经音频编解码器。该编解码器将音频转换为 12.5 Hz 的潜在 token，包含语义和声学两个组件。

Pavan explained that unlike text where there's a clear "winner" architecture, audio is still evolving. "There is no winner model yet... people are still iterating and figuring out what's the best overall recipe." The flow-matching approach was chosen specifically for real-time streaming — a primary use case being voice agents.

Pavan 解释道，与文本领域已有明确的"赢家"架构不同，音频领域仍在演进。"还没有赢家模型……人们仍在迭代并 figuring out 最佳的整体方案。"选择 flow-matching 方法 specifically 是为了实时流式传输——主要应用场景是语音 agent。

Guillaume also discussed Mistral's broader strategy. They're strong believers in "AI for science" — pairing AI researchers with domain experts in physics and material science to solve problems no one else is working on. They also talked about their "forward deployed" engineering model, where applied scientists work directly with customers to fine-tune models for specific use cases, creating a feedback loop that improves the base model itself. "It's a real world eval," Guillaume noted. "There is a fairly big gap between the public benchmarks that are fairly academic and what customers actually need."

Guillaume 还讨论了 Mistral 的更广泛战略。他们坚信"AI for science"——将 AI 研究人员与物理学和材料科学领域的专家配对，解决其他人没有在做的问题。他们还谈到了"forward deployed"工程模式，应用科学家直接与客户合作，针对特定用例微调模型，形成改进基础模型本身的反馈循环。"这是真实世界的评估，"Guillaume 指出。"公开基准测试相当学术化，与客户实际需求之间存在相当大的差距。"
