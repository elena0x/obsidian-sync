### The MAD Podcast with Matt Turck: OpenAI's Dan Roberts — Why AI Can Now Make Discoveries

**The Takeaway:** The real breakthrough isn't just bigger models — it's reinforcement learning (RL) enabling AI to discover solutions humans haven't found, by learning through doing rather than watching demonstrations.

OpenAI researcher Dan Roberts sits down with Matt Turck to explain how AI cracked mathematical problems that had stumped humans for decades, particularly the Erdos problems. The secret sauce isn't scale — it's RL, the paradigm of learning by trial and error rather than imitation. Roberts makes a compelling case that intelligence is becoming customizable, not just bigger. The magic happens when you combine pre-training with RL: pre-training gives you broad knowledge, RL gives you the ability to push into unknown territory. He also contrasts approaches — OpenAI's informal, human-readable proof style versus DeepMind's formal Lean-based verification — suggesting there's room for both philosophies in advancing AI-driven mathematics.
