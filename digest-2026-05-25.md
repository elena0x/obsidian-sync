**Latent Space: Mistral — Voxtral TTS, Forge, Leanstral, & what's next for Mistral 4**

**The Takeaway:** Mistral is betting that specialized, efficient models beat generalist giants — and they're open-sourcing their way there, from a 3B-parameter TTS model to a formal math prover.

**核心要点：** Mistral 押注专业化、高效的模型胜过通用大模型——他们通过开源实现这一目标，从 30 亿参数的 TTS 模型到形式化数学证明器。

Mistral Chief Scientist Guillaume Lample and audio research lead Pavan Kumar Reddy joined Latent Space to discuss three major releases: Voxtral TTS (a 3B-parameter text-to-speech model supporting 9 languages), Mistral Small (a merged multimodal model combining coding, vision, and reasoning capabilities), and Leanstral (a formal math prover built on Lean).

Mistral 首席科学家 Guillaume Lample 和音频研究负责人 Pavan Kumar Reddy 做客 Latent Space，讨论了三大发布：Voxtral TTS（支持 9 种语言的 30 亿参数文本转语音模型）、Mistral Small（融合编码、视觉和推理能力的多模态合并模型）、以及 Leanstral（基于 Lean 构建的形式化数学证明器）。

On Voxtral TTS, Pavan explained their novel architecture: an autoregressive flow matching model with an in-house neural audio codec that converts audio into 12.5 Hz latent tokens (semantic + acoustic). Unlike depth transformers that require K autoregressive steps per frame, flow matching cuts inference to just 4-16 steps — critical for real-time voice agents. "Even the same word with your own voice could be inflected in so many different ways... you have to pick one and then sharp." The model is open-weight and costs "a fraction" of competitors.

关于 Voxtral TTS，Pavan 解释了他们的新颖架构：自回归流匹配模型，配合自研神经音频编解码器，将音频转换为 12.5 Hz 的潜在 token（语义+声学）。与每帧需要 K 步自回归的深度 Transformer 不同，流匹配将推理缩减至仅 4-16 步——这对实时语音 Agent 至关重要。"即使是你自己的声音，同一个词也可以有那么多不同的语调变化……你必须选一个，然后把它锐化。"该模型是开放权重的，成本仅为竞争对手的"一小部分"。

Guillaume emphasized Mistral's "capability-by-capability" strategy: teams build specialized models (transcription, TTS, vision, coding), then merge the mature ones into a generalist — but only when it makes sense. "If you just want to transcribe text, it makes no sense to use a model this large... you probably just want a 1B or 3B model." This explains why they release separate efficient models alongside generalists.

Guillaume 强调了 Mistral 的"逐个能力"策略：团队先构建专门的模型（转录、TTS、视觉、编码），然后将成熟的模型合并到通用模型中——但只在有意义的时候这样做。"如果你只是想转录文本，用这么大的模型毫无意义……你可能只需要 10 亿或 30 亿参数的模型。"这解释了为什么他们在发布通用模型的同时，还会发布独立的高效模型。

On Leanstral, Guillaume framed formal proving as the ultimate verifiable reasoning task — unlike math competition problems where solutions are just numbers, proofs have no easy verification. "If it compiles in Lean, it's correct." He sees this as a proxy for long-horizon reasoning and planning that could generalize across domains, and anticipates coding agents will dramatically expand formal verification in industries like aerospace where software correctness is life-critical.

关于 Leanstral，Guillaume 将形式化证明视为终极可验证的推理任务——与数学竞赛题（答案只是数字）不同，证明没有简单的验证方式。"如果它在 Lean 中编译通过，它就是正确的。"他认为这是长程推理和规划的代理，可能跨领域泛化，并预计编码 Agent 将大幅扩展航空航天等行业的形式化验证，在这些行业中软件正确性关乎生死。

Mistral is hiring aggressively across Paris, London, Palo Alto, Warsaw, Zurich, New York, and soon San Francisco — but intends to stay "a fairly small team" to maintain agility.

Mistral 正在巴黎、伦敦、帕洛阿尔托、华沙、苏黎世、纽约和即将开设的旧金山办公室积极招聘——但打算保持"相当小的团队"以维持敏捷性。
