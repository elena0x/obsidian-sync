### Aaron Levie (Box CEO)

Levie argues that AI agents haven't eliminated work — they've moved it up a layer of abstraction. The new work is figuring out what to tell the agent, giving it proper instructions and context, intervening when it goes off task, reviewing output, and incorporating it into something larger. The longer the task horizon, the more planning and review stages take up. "You are now the editor. A manager. A producer. The taste matters, understanding the craft matters, and the skills ultimately matter." Agents automate the annoying parts of work and keep the more enjoyable parts — but the work is still there, just different.

Box CEO Aaron Levie 认为 AI agent 并没有消除工作，而是将工作提升到了更高层次的抽象。新的工作是：想清楚要告诉 agent 做什么、给它正确的指令和上下文、在它偏离任务时干预、审查最终产出、并将其整合到更大的成果中。任务周期越长，规划和审查阶段占的比重就越大。"你现在就是编辑、管理者、制片人。品味很重要，理解 craftsmanship 很重要，技能最终也很重要。" Agent 自动化了工作中烦人的部分，保留了更有趣的部分 —— 但工作依然存在，只是形式不同了。

### Latent Space: Mistral — Voxtral TTS, Forge, Leanstral, & what's next for Mistral 4

**The Takeaway:** Mistral is betting that the future of AI isn't one giant model that does everything — it's a portfolio of specialized, efficient models tailored to specific use cases, deployed on-premise where data privacy matters most.

**核心要点：** Mistral 押注 AI 的未来不是做一个能做所有事的巨型模型，而是一个由针对特定用例优化的专业高效模型组成的组合，在数据隐私最重要的场景下本地部署。

Mistral Chief Scientist Guillaume Lample and Audio Research Lead Pavan Kumar Reddy joined Latent Space to discuss three major releases: Voxtral TTS (text-to-speech), Mistral Small (their new unified multimodal model), and Leanstral (their formal math reasoning model).

Mistral 首席科学家 Guillaume Lample 和音频研究负责人 Pavan Kumar Reddy 做客 Latent Space，讨论了三大发布：Voxtral TTS（文本转语音）、Mistral Small（新的统一多模态模型）和 Leanstral（形式化数学推理模型）。

On Voxtral TTS: It's a 3B parameter model based on their Ministral backbone, supporting 9 languages. What makes it novel is the architecture — they developed an autoregressive flow matching approach with an in-house neural audio codec that converts audio into 12.5 Hz latent tokens (semantic + acoustic). This replaces the traditional "depth transformer" approach that requires K autoregressive steps per time frame, significantly reducing latency. Pavan noted that unlike text where one word maps to one token, audio has far more entropy — the same word can be inflected countless ways. Flow matching models this distribution better than discrete token approaches. The model is open weights and designed for real-time voice agent applications.

关于 Voxtral TTS：这是一个基于 Ministral 骨干网络的 30 亿参数模型，支持 9 种语言。其创新之处在于架构 —— 他们开发了一种自回归流匹配（autoregressive flow matching）方法，配合自研的神经音频编解码器，将音频转换为 12.5 Hz 的潜在 token（语义 + 声学）。这取代了传统的"深度 transformer"方法（每个时间帧需要 K 个自回归步骤），显著降低了延迟。Pavan 指出，与文本中一个词对应一个 token 不同，音频的熵要高得多 —— 同一个词可以有无数种语调变化。流匹配比离散 token 方法更好地建模了这种分布。该模型以开放权重发布，专为实时语音 agent 应用设计。

On Mistral Small: It's a sparse MoE with 6B active parameters and 256K context window. For the first time, Mistral merged all their specialized models (coding/Devstral, reasoning/Magistral, vision/Pixtral) into one unified model. Lample explained their development philosophy: individual teams focus on one capability, build one model, and once mature enough, they merge it into the main model. He pushed back on the "one giant omni model" vision, arguing that for specific use cases like transcription, a 1B or 3B specialized model performs essentially the same as a massive generalist model at a fraction of the cost.

关于 Mistral Small：这是一个稀疏 MoE 模型，60 亿活跃参数，256K 上下文窗口。Mistral 首次将所有专业模型（编程/Devstral、推理/Magistral、视觉/Pixtral）合并为一个统一模型。Lample 解释了他们的开发哲学：各个团队专注于一个能力，构建一个模型，成熟后合并到主模型中。他反对"一个巨型全能模型"的愿景，认为对于转录等特定用例，10 亿或 30 亿参数的专业模型在成本大幅降低的情况下，性能与巨型通用模型基本相同。

On the business model: Mistral emphasized their hands-on approach with enterprise customers. They help companies deploy models on-premise or private cloud for data privacy, and their Forge platform enables fine-tuning on proprietary domain data. Lample made a strong case: "Using closed source models is really sad because it puts you on the same model as all your competitors when you could be using everything you've collected for years." They shared examples of building custom models for rare languages (50% of training mix vs. 0.1% in general models) and offline voice agents for automotive use cases.

关于商业模式：Mistral 强调了他们与企业客户的深度合作。他们帮助公司在本地或私有云部署模型以确保数据隐私，Forge 平台支持在专有领域数据上进行微调。Lample 提出了一个有力论点："使用闭源模型真的很可惜，因为这让你和所有竞争对手用同样的模型，而你本可以利用多年来收集的所有数据。"他们分享了为稀有语言构建定制模型（训练混合比例 50% vs 通用模型的 0.1%）以及为汽车场景构建离线语音 agent 的案例。

On Leanstral: This is Mistral's foray into formal mathematical proving using Lean. Lample explained that while current reasoning models work well on verifiable problems (like AIME where answers are numbers), most real-world reasoning lacks easy verification. Formal proving systems like Lean solve this — if it compiles, it's correct. This avoids the reward hacking and judge-model limitations of LLM-as-judge approaches.

关于 Leanstral：这是 Mistral 使用 Lean 进行形式化数学证明的尝试。Lample 解释说，虽然当前的推理模型在可验证的问题上表现良好（如 AIME，答案是数字），但大多数现实世界的推理缺乏简单的验证方式。Lean 等形式化证明系统解决了这个问题 —— 如果能编译通过，就是正确的。这避免了 LLM 作为评判者时的奖励黑客和模型评判局限性。

On open source: Lample reaffirmed that open sourcing is "in the DNA of the company." He traced it back to their work on LLaMA at Meta, which democratized LLM research. "We really don't want to live in a world where the smartest models are only behind closed doors, only accessible to a few companies that have the power to decide who can use them or not."

关于开源：Lample 重申开源"是公司的 DNA"。他追溯到他们在 Meta 时做的 LLaMA 工作，这项工作使 LLM 研究民主化。"我们真的不想生活在一个最聪明的模型只被关在门后、只有少数公司能决定谁可以使用它们的世界里。"
