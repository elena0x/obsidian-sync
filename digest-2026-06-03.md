### Aaron Levie (CEO at Box)

Aaron Levie delivered a detailed thread on why AI agents don't eliminate work — they transform it. When agents do work for you, your job shifts up a layer of abstraction: figuring out what to tell the agent, giving proper instructions, providing context, intervening when off-task, reviewing output, and incorporating it into broader work. "You are now the editor. A manager. A producer. The taste matters, understanding the craft matters, and the skills ultimately matter." He argues AI automates the annoying parts while keeping the enjoyable parts — but the work is still there, just different.

Box CEO Aaron Levie 发表长文解释为什么 AI agent 不会消除工作，而是改变工作。当 agent 替你干活时，你的工作上升了一层抽象：你要想清楚让 agent 做什么、给正确的指令、提供上下文、在跑偏时干预、审核最终产出、并将其整合到更大的工作中。"你现在成了编辑、经理、制片人。品味很重要，理解手艺很重要，技能最终仍然重要。"他认为 AI 自动化的是烦人的部分，留下的是有趣的部分——但工作还在，只是形式不同了。

### Latent Space: Mistral — Voxtral TTS, Forge, Leanstral, & what's next for Mistral 4

**The Takeaway:** Mistral is aggressively expanding beyond LLMs into voice (Voxtral TTS), developer tools (Forge), and leaner deployment options (Leanstral) — positioning itself as a full-stack AI infrastructure company, not just a model provider.

Mistral 正在积极从 LLM 领域向外扩张，进军语音（Voxtral TTS）、开发者工具（Forge）和更轻量的部署方案（Leanstral）——将自己定位为全栈 AI 基础设施公司，而不仅仅是模型提供商。

In this episode, Pavan Kumar Reddy and Guillaume Lample from Mistral discuss the company's evolution from a European LLM challenger to a comprehensive AI platform. Voxtral represents their entry into text-to-speech, competing directly with ElevenLabs and OpenAI's voice offerings. Forge is a new developer framework designed to make building with Mistral models significantly easier — a clear response to the success of tools like Vercel's AI SDK and LangChain. Leanstral addresses the enterprise demand for running smaller, more efficient models on-premise or at the edge, acknowledging that not every use case needs a 70B parameter model.

本期节目中，Mistral 的 Pavan Kumar Reddy 和 Guillaume Lample 讨论了公司从欧洲 LLM 挑战者到综合 AI 平台的演变。Voxtral 标志着他们进入文本转语音领域，直接与 ElevenLabs 和 OpenAI 的语音产品竞争。Forge 是一个新的开发者框架，旨在让使用 Mistral 模型构建应用变得更加简单——这是对 Vercel AI SDK 和 LangChain 等工具成功的明确回应。Leanstral 则满足企业在本地或边缘端运行更小、更高效模型的需求，承认并非每个用例都需要 700 亿参数的模型。

The conversation reveals Mistral's strategic bet: while OpenAI and Anthropic compete on raw model capability, Mistral is building the surrounding infrastructure — voice, tools, deployment flexibility — that makes their models actually usable in production. Lample's background as a co-author of the original Transformer paper lends credibility to their technical direction.
