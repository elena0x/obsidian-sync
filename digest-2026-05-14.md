### Aaron Levie (CEO, Box)

Levie argues that AI agents haven't eliminated work — they've moved it up a layer of abstraction. "You are now the editor. A manager. A producer. The taste matters, understanding the craft matters, and the skills ultimately matter."

Box CEO Aaron Levie 认为 AI Agent 并没有消除工作，而是将工作提升了一层抽象。新的工作内容是：想清楚告诉 Agent 做什么、给它正确的指令和上下文、在它偏离任务时干预、审阅输出并将其整合到其他工作中。「你现在就是编辑、管理者、制片人。品味很重要，理解手艺很重要，技能最终也很重要。」

### Latent Space: Mistral — Voxtral TTS, Forge, Mistral Small, & What's Next

**The Takeaway:** Mistral is taking a deliberately different path from the "one giant model does everything" approach, betting instead on a portfolio of specialized, efficient models — and they're releasing the weights.

Mistral 正在采取一条与「一个巨型模型做所有事」截然不同的路径，押注于一系列专业化、高效的模型组合——并且他们正在开源权重。

Mistral Chief Scientist Guillaume Lample and Audio Research Lead Pavan Kumar Reddy joined Latent Space to discuss three major releases: Voxtral TTS (a 3B-parameter text-to-speech model), the unified Mistral Small model (merging coding, vision, and text capabilities), and Leanstral (a model for formal mathematical proving).

Mistral 首席科学家 Guillaume Lample 和音频研究负责人 Pavan Kumar Reddy 做客 Latent Space，讨论了三大发布：Voxtral TTS（30 亿参数的文本转语音模型）、统一版 Mistral Small（融合编码、视觉和文本能力）、以及 Leanstral（用于形式化数学证明的模型）。

On Voxtral TTS, Pavan explained their novel architecture: an autoregressive flow-matching approach with an in-house neural audio codec that converts audio into 12.5 Hz latent tokens. Unlike competitors that use depth transformers requiring K autoregressive steps per time frame, Mistral's flow-matching head cuts inference down to just 4-16 steps. "Even at a particular time step, there is a distribution of things to be predicted... you can't predict the mean of it because that corresponds to some blurred out speech." The model supports 9 languages and is designed for real-time voice agents.

关于 Voxtral TTS，Pavan 解释了他们的新颖架构：一种自回归流匹配方法，搭配自研神经音频编解码器，将音频转换为 12.5 Hz 的潜在 token。与竞争对手每时间帧需要 K 个自回归步骤的深度 Transformer 不同，Mistral 的流匹配头将推理缩减至仅 4-16 步。「即使在特定时间步，也存在一个需要预测的分布……你不能预测它的均值，因为那对应的是模糊的语音。」该模型支持 9 种语言，专为实时语音 Agent 设计。

Guillaume emphasized Mistral's enterprise strategy: helping customers deploy models on-premise or in private cloud for data privacy, then fine-tuning on their proprietary data. "If you actually fine tune, you can actually really really go much further... The model is trained on your entire company knowledge, so it knows everything." He claimed they can often build solutions 10x cheaper than closed-source alternatives through targeted fine-tuning.

Guillaume 强调了 Mistral 的企业战略：帮助客户在本地或私有云部署模型以确保数据隐私，然后在其专有数据上进行微调。「如果你真正进行微调，你可以走得更远……模型训练于你整个公司的知识，所以它什么都知道。」他声称通过针对性微调，他们通常可以比闭源替代方案便宜 10 倍地构建解决方案。

On open source, Guillaume was unequivocal: "We really don't want to be living in a world where the smartest model, the best models are only behind closed doors... We want intelligence to be used and accessible by anyone."

关于开源，Guillaume 态度明确：「我们真的不想生活在一个最聪明、最好的模型只关在门后的世界里……我们希望任何人都能使用并获得智能。」
