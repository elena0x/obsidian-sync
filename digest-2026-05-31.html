Roblox Product Lead Peter Yang on the Future of Work in an AI Agent World

Peter Yang (Product at Roblox) shared five key takeaways from his conversation with a16z GP Anish Acharya about how AI agents will reshape work: 1) Coding agents will eat all knowledge work — the first 80% of docs, slides, and analytics can now be done by AI before human polish; 2) Small 2-3 person teams with agent swarms will outperform bloated orgs; 3) Task-completion apps will shrink while entertainment apps endure; 4) Personal agents will understand us deeply — Yang's OpenClaw once reminded him to prioritize his kids over career; 5) Human ambition has no ceiling — expect more one-person companies as big tech layoffs continue. https://x.com/petergyang/status/2041331383344443795
Roblox 产品负责人 Peter Yang 与 a16z 合伙人 Anish Acharya 对谈，分享 AI Agent 时代工作的五大趋势：1）Coding Agent 将吞噬所有知识工作；2）2-3 人的小团队加 Agent 群将击败臃肿大组织；3）完成任务型应用会萎缩，娱乐应用长存；4）个人 Agent 将深度理解你；5）人类野心无上限，一人公司时代来临。 https://x.com/petergyang/status/2041331383344443795

Box CEO Aaron Levie on AI Agents and the Nature of Work

Levie argues that AI agents haven't eliminated work — they've moved it up a layer of abstraction. The new work is: figuring out what to tell the agent, giving proper instructions, providing context, intervening when it goes off-task, reviewing output, and incorporating it into something else. "You are now the editor. A manager. A producer. The taste matters, understanding the craft matters, and the skills ultimately matter." Agents automate the annoying parts and keep the enjoyable ones. https://x.com/levie/status/2041347596342460439
Box CEO Aaron Levie 认为 AI Agent 并没有消除工作，而是将其提升了一个抽象层级。新的工作包括：告诉 Agent 做什么、提供正确指令、给足上下文、在偏离时干预、审查最终产出。"你现在是编辑、经理、制片人。品味很重要，对手艺的理解很重要，技能最终很重要。" https://x.com/levie/status/2041347596342460439
