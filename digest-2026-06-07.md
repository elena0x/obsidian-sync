### Aaron Levie (CEO @ Box)

Aaron Levie wrote a thoughtful thread pushing back on the idea that AI agents eliminate work. He argues that when agents do work for you, the work simply moves up a layer of abstraction: now your job is figuring out what to tell the agent, giving it proper instructions, providing context, intervening when it goes off-task, reviewing output, and incorporating it into something else. The longer the task, the more planning and review stages take up. "You are now the editor. A manager. A producer." AI hasn't eliminated work — it has transformed it, automating the annoying parts while keeping the more enjoyable, higher-level ones.

Aaron Levie 写了一篇深思熟虑的 thread，反驳 AI agent 消除工作的观点。他认为，当 agent 为你完成工作时，工作只是向上移动了一层抽象：现在你的工作是 figuring out 如何给 agent 指令、提供 proper instructions、给予 context、在 agent 偏离任务时干预、review 输出并将其整合到其他事物中。任务越长，planning 和 review 阶段就越耗时。"你现在是编辑。是经理。是制作人。" AI 没有消除工作——它改变了工作，自动化了烦人的部分，同时保留了更有趣、更高层次的部分。

### Latent Space: Mistral — Voxtral TTS, Forge, Leanstral, & what's next for Mistral 4

**The Takeaway:** Mistral is systematically expanding from text into multimodal AI, with their new Voxtral TTS model representing a deliberate step toward building a full "speech-to-speech" foundation model that could eventually power real-time conversational AI agents.

**The Takeaway / 核心要点：** Mistral 正在系统性地从文本扩展到多模态 AI，其新的 Voxtral TTS 模型代表了向构建完整"语音到语音"基础模型迈出的有意一步，最终可能为实时对话 AI agent 提供动力。

Mistral's chief scientist Guillaume Lample and audio research lead Pavan Kumar Reddy joined the Latent Space podcast to discuss their latest releases. Voxtral TTS is Mistral's first speech-generation model — a natural extension of their earlier transcription (ASR) work. It supports nine languages, runs on a compact 3B parameter architecture, and achieves quality comparable to top competitors at a fraction of the cost. Notably, the weights are being released openly, though not under an Apache license this time — a strategic decision signaling more open-weight releases to come.

Mistral 首席科学家 Guillaume Lample 和音频研究负责人 Pavan Kumar Reddy 加入 Latent Space 播客讨论他们最新的发布。Voxtral TTS 是 Mistral 首个语音生成模型——是其早期转录（ASR）工作的自然延伸。它支持九种语言，运行在紧凑的 3B 参数架构上，质量可与顶级竞争对手媲美，但成本仅为后者的一小部分。值得注意的是，模型权重将开放发布，但此次不采用 Apache 许可证——这是一个战略决策，暗示未来将有更多开放权重发布。

The conversation also touched on Forge (Mistral's agentic framework) and Leanstral (their efficiency-focused model series). The Mistral team emphasized that all their foundation model evaluations are "just proxies" — the real test is how models perform in actual use cases. They're building toward a future where speech input and output are natively integrated into a single model, rather than chaining separate ASR → LLM → TTS pipelines. This "full circle" approach could dramatically simplify real-time voice agents and reduce latency.

对话还涉及 Forge（Mistral 的 agentic 框架）和 Leanstral（其专注效率的模型系列）。Mistral 团队强调，他们所有的基础模型评估都只是"代理指标"——真正的考验是模型在实际用例中的表现。他们正在构建一个未来，其中语音输入和输出被原生集成到单一模型中，而不是链接单独的 ASR → LLM → TTS 管道。这种"完整闭环"方法可能大幅简化实时语音 agent 并降低延迟。
