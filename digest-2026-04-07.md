Box CEO Aaron Levie 认为 AI agent 并没有消除工作，而是将工作提升了一个抽象层级。新的工作变成了：想清楚要告诉 agent 做什么、给出正确指令、提供上下文、在 agent 偏离时干预、审查最终产出、并将其整合到其他事物中。"你现在成了编辑、管理者、制片人。品味很重要，理解你所从事的技艺很重要，技能最终也很重要。"

### Latent Space: Mistral — Voxtral TTS, Forge, Leanstral, & what's next for Mistral 4

**The Takeaway:** Mistral is betting on specialized, efficient models over giant generalists — their new Voxtral TTS is a 3B open-weights speech generation model that runs at a fraction of the cost of competitors while matching top-tier quality, built on a novel autoregressive flow-matching architecture with an in-house neural audio codec.

**核心要点：** Mistral 押注于专业化、高效模型而非巨型通用模型——他们新的 Voxtral TTS 是一个 30 亿参数的开放权重语音生成模型，成本仅为竞争对手的一小部分，同时质量达到顶级水平，基于新颖的自回归流匹配架构和自研神经音频编解码器构建。

Mistral Chief Scientist Guillaume Lample and audio research lead Pavan Kumar Reddy joined Latent Space to discuss their latest releases. Voxtral TTS supports nine languages and is built on their Ministral 3B backbone with a novel flow-matching head instead of traditional discrete token prediction. Pavan explained that unlike text where one word maps to one token, audio has enormous entropy — the same word can be inflected countless ways. Flow matching models this distribution better than depth transformers, and cuts inference from K autoregressive steps down to just 4-16 steps, enabling real-time streaming for voice agents.

Mistral 首席科学家 Guillaume Lample 和音频研究负责人 Pavan Kumar Reddy 做客 Latent Space，讨论了他们最新的发布。Voxtral TTS 支持九种语言，基于 Ministral 3B 主干网络，采用新颖的流匹配头而非传统的离散 token 预测。Pavan 解释说，与文本中一个词对应一个 token 不同，音频具有巨大的熵——同一个词可以有无数种语调变化。流匹配比深度变换器更好地建模这种分布，并将推理从 K 个自回归步骤减少到仅 4-16 步，为语音 agent 实现实时流式传输。

Guillaume emphasized Mistral's strategy of building specialized models for specific use cases rather than one expensive generalist. He also discussed their enterprise platform Forge, which helps customers deploy models on-premise, fine-tune on their proprietary data, and build custom solutions — sometimes 10x cheaper than closed-source alternatives. On open source, Guillaume stated: "We really don't want to be living in a world where the smartest models are only behind closed doors, only accessible to a few companies that have the power to decide who can use them or not."

Guillaume 强调了 Mistral 为特定用例构建专业化模型而非一个昂贵的通用模型的策略。他还讨论了他们的企业平台 Forge，帮助客户在本地部署模型、在其专有数据上进行微调，并构建定制解决方案——有时比闭源替代方案便宜 10 倍。关于开源，Guillaume 表示："我们真的不想生活在一个最聪明的模型只被关在门后、只有少数有权力决定谁可以使用它们的公司才能接触到的世界里。"

They also touched on Leanstral — Mistral's entry into formal mathematical proving using Lean. Guillaume explained that while current reasoning models work well on verifiable problems (like AIME math competitions), most real-world reasoning lacks an easy correctness check. Lean solves this: if the proof compiles, it's correct. The challenge is the tiny dataset, but Mistral sees it as a path to genuine reasoning capabilities.

他们还提到了 Leanstral——Mistral 使用 Lean 进行形式化数学证明的尝试。Guillaume 解释说，虽然当前的推理模型在可验证的问题上表现良好（如 AIME 数学竞赛），但大多数现实世界的推理缺乏简单的正确性检查。Lean 解决了这个问题：如果证明编译通过，它就是正确的。挑战在于数据集极小，但 Mistral 将其视为通往真正推理能力的路径。
