### The MAD Podcast with Matt Turck — OpenAI's Dan Roberts: Why AI Can Now Make Discoveries

OpenAI researcher Dan Roberts discusses AI's breakthrough in solving mathematical problems, particularly the Erdős problems. He explains reinforcement learning as the key paradigm, comparing it to learning by doing versus watching demonstrations. Roberts argues that intelligence is becoming customizable rather than just about bigger models, and that the combination of pre-training plus RL is what makes modern AI systems so powerful. He also discusses the difference between OpenAI's informal approach to math proofs versus DeepMind's formal Lean-based approach.
