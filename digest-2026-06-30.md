### The MAD Podcast with Matt Turck — OpenAI's Dan Roberts: Why AI Can Now Make Discoveries

**The Takeaway:** Reinforcement learning, not just scale, is what finally enabled AI to solve mathematical problems that humans couldn't.

OpenAI researcher Dan Roberts explains how AI made a breakthrough in solving mathematical problems, particularly the Erdős problems. The key paradigm shift is reinforcement learning — learning by doing rather than watching demonstrations. Roberts argues that intelligence is becoming customizable rather than just about bigger models, and that the combination of pre-training plus RL is what makes modern AI systems so powerful. He contrasts OpenAI's informal approach to math proofs with DeepMind's formal Lean-based approach.
