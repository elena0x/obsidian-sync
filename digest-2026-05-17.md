### Aaron Levie (CEO @ Box)

Aaron Levie wrote a long thread arguing that AI agents haven't eliminated work — they've moved it up a layer of abstraction. The new work is figuring out what to tell the agent, giving proper instructions, providing context, intervening when it goes off-task, reviewing output, and incorporating it into something else. "You are now the editor. A manager. A producer. The taste matters, understanding the craft matters, and the skills ultimately matter." He concludes that agents automate the annoying parts and keep the more enjoyable parts, but the work is still there, just different.

Aaron Levie 发长文指出 AI agent 并没有消除工作，而是将工作提升了一层抽象。新的工作变成了：告诉 agent 做什么、给出正确指令、提供上下文、在偏离时干预、审阅输出、整合结果。"你现在就是编辑、经理、制片人。品味很重要，理解手艺很重要，技能最终很重要。"他总结说 agent 自动化了烦人的部分，保留了更有趣的部分，但工作依然存在，只是形式不同。

### Latent Space: Mistral — Voxtral TTS, Forge, Mistral Small, & what's next for Mistral 4

**The Takeaway:** Mistral is betting that audio is the next frontier where open-source can win — they're releasing Voxtral TTS, a 3B-parameter text-to-speech model that matches the best closed competitors at a fraction of the cost, with open weights and support for 9 languages.

**核心要点：** Mistral 押注音频是开源可以获胜的下一个前沿——他们发布了 Voxtral TTS，一个 30 亿参数的文本转语音模型，以极低的成本媲美最好的闭源竞品，支持 9 种语言并开放权重。

Mistral Chief Scientist Guillaume Lample and Head of Audio Research Pavan Kumar Reddy joined the Latent Space podcast to discuss their latest releases. The big announcement is Voxtral TTS — Mistral's first speech generation model, built on a novel autoregressive flow-matching architecture with an in-house neural audio codec that converts audio into 12.5 Hertz latent tokens (semantic + acoustic). At just 3B parameters, it's designed for real-time voice agents and is "one of the best open source models" according to the team.

Mistral 首席科学家 Guillaume Lample 和音频研究负责人 Pavan Kumar Reddy 做客 Latent Space 播客，讨论了他们的最新发布。最大的消息是 Voxtral TTS——Mistral 首个语音生成模型，基于新颖的自回归流匹配架构，配合自研神经音频编解码器，将音频转换为 12.5 赫兹的潜在 token（语义+声学）。仅 30 亿参数，专为实时语音 agent 设计，团队称这是"最好的开源模型之一"。

The conversation also covered Mistral Small — their new merged model combining instruction following, coding (Devstral), math (Magistral), and vision capabilities into one sparse 6B-active-parameter model with 256K context. Unlike competitors building one giant generalist model, Mistral's strategy is to build specialized models for specific use cases (transcription, TTS, voice agents) that are far more efficient and cost-effective.

对话还涉及了 Mistral Small——他们的新合并模型，将指令遵循、编程（Devstral）、数学（Magistral）和视觉能力整合到一个仅 6B 活跃参数的稀疏模型中，支持 256K 上下文。与竞争对手构建一个巨型通用模型不同，Mistral 的策略是为特定用例（转录、TTS、语音 agent）构建专门模型，效率更高、成本更低。

On the open-source philosophy, Lample was direct: "We really don't want to be living in a world where the smartest models are only behind closed doors... It's a scary future we don't want to live in." They also teased Leanstral — a formal math proving model — and hinted that the eventual goal is a full-duplex audio model that can speak while listening.

关于开源理念，Lample 直言："我们真的不想生活在一个最聪明的模型只关在门后的世界里……那是一个我们不想面对的可怕未来。"他们还预告了 Leanstral——一个形式化数学证明模型，并暗示最终目标是实现全双工音频模型，能边说边听。
