### Aaron Levie (CEO @Box)

Levie argues that AI agents haven't eliminated work — they've shifted it up a layer of abstraction. The new work is: figuring out what to tell the agent, giving proper instructions, providing context, intervening when it goes off-task, reviewing output, and incorporating it into the next thing. "You are now the editor. A manager. A producer. The taste matters, understanding the craft matters, and the skills ultimately matter." The longer the task, the more planning and review stages consume. Agents automate the annoying parts, but the work is still there — just different.

### Latent Space — Mistral: Voxtral TTS, Forge, Leanstral, & what's next for Mistral 4

**The Takeaway:** Mistral is betting on a modular, capability-specific model strategy rather than one giant omni-model — and they're open-sourcing the weights to keep intelligence accessible to everyone.

Guillaume Lample (Chief Scientist) and Pavan Kumar Reddy (Audio Research Lead) joined Latent Space to discuss Mistral's new Voxtral TTS speech-generation model, the Leanstral formal-math model, and their broader vision. Voxtral is a 3B-parameter model supporting 9 languages, built on an autoregressive flow-matching architecture with an in-house neural audio codec. It's designed for real-time voice agents, with inference latency significantly lower than depth-transformer alternatives. The model is open-weight, though not fully open-source this time.

On Leanstral, Lample explained the motivation: formal proof systems like Lean offer verifiable correctness, which is a natural fit for RL training. Unlike math problems where you can check the final answer, most real-world reasoning lacks an easy verifier. Lean compilations provide one. Mistral sees this as a path to long-horizon reasoning and planning — skills that transfer to coding and other domains.

Mistral's broader strategy is to build specialized models for specific use cases (transcription, TTS, vision, coding, reasoning) and merge them into a generalist over time — rather than starting with one massive model that does everything inefficiently. They also emphasized their "forward deployed" engineering team, which works directly with enterprise customers to fine-tune models on proprietary data, often achieving 10x cost reductions compared to closed-source alternatives.

**Latent Space — Mistral: Voxtral TTS, Forge, Leanstral 与 Mistral 4 的下一步**

**核心要点：** Mistral 押注于模块化、能力专精的模型策略，而非一个万能大模型 —— 并且他们坚持开源权重，让智能技术对所有人可及。

首席科学家 Guillaume Lample 和音频研究负责人 Pavan Kumar Reddy 做客 Latent Space，讨论了 Mistral 的新 Voxtral TTS 语音生成模型、Leanstral 形式数学模型，以及他们的整体愿景。Voxtral 是一个 30 亿参数、支持 9 种语言的模型，基于自回归流匹配架构和自研神经音频编解码器。它为实时语音 agent 设计，推理延迟显著低于深度 Transformer 替代方案。该模型开放权重，但这次并非完全开源。

关于 Leanstral，Lample 解释了动机：像 Lean 这样的形式证明系统提供可验证的正确性，天然适合 RL 训练。与可以核对最终答案的数学题不同，大多数现实世界推理缺乏简单的验证器。Lean 编译提供了这样一个验证器。Mistral 将其视为长程推理和规划能力的路径 —— 这些技能可迁移到编码和其他领域。

Mistral 的更广泛策略是为特定用例构建专精模型（转录、TTS、视觉、编码、推理），然后逐步合并为通用模型 —— 而非从一个低效地做所有事的巨型模型开始。他们还强调了「前沿部署」工程团队，直接与企业客户合作，在专有数据上微调模型，通常比闭源替代方案实现 10 倍成本降低。
