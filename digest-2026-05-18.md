### Aaron Levie (Box CEO)

Aaron Levie pushed back on the idea that AI agents eliminate work entirely. When agents do work for you, the work just moves up a layer of abstraction — now you're figuring out what to tell the agent, ensuring proper instructions, providing context, intervening when it goes off-task, reviewing output, and incorporating it elsewhere. Any one of these components being off and "poof you will have useless work product." Even as agents complete longer-horizon tasks, planning and review stages don't go away — in fact they take up more time. "You are now the editor. A manager. A producer. The taste matters, understanding the craft matters, and the skills ultimately matter." AI agents automate the annoying parts of work and keep the more enjoyable parts — but the work is still there, just different.

Aaron Levie 反驳了 AI agent 会彻底消除工作的观点。当 agent 替你干活时，工作只是向上抽象了一层 —— 你现在要思考如何给 agent 下指令、确保指令准确、提供上下文、在 agent 跑偏时干预、审阅最终产出、并将其整合到别处。任何一个环节出错，"砰 —— 你就得到了一堆无用的工作成果"。即使 agent 能完成更长周期的任务，规划和审阅环节也不会消失 —— 事实上它们会占用更多时间。"你现在成了编辑、经理、制片人。品味很重要，理解手艺很重要，技能最终也很重要。"AI agent 自动化了工作中烦人的部分，保留了更有趣的部分 —— 但工作依然存在，只是形式不同。

### Latent Space: Mistral — Voxtral TTS, Forge, Leanstral, & What's Next

**The Takeaway:** Mistral is betting that specialized, efficient models beat generalist giants — and they're releasing open weights to prove it.

Mistral Chief Scientist Guillaume Lample and Audio Research Lead Pavan Kumar Reddy joined Latent Space to discuss their latest releases. The headline is Voxtral TTS, a 3B parameter text-to-speech model that matches top competitors at a fraction of the cost — with open weights. Pavan revealed the novel architecture: an autoregressive flow matching approach with an in-house neural audio codec that converts audio into 12.5 Hertz latent tokens (semantic + acoustic). This replaces the traditional depth transformer with a more efficient flow matching head, cutting latency significantly while maintaining quality.

Mistral 首席科学家 Guillaume Lample 和音频研究负责人 Pavan Kumar Reddy 做客 Latent Space，讨论了他们的最新发布。最引人注目的是 Voxtral TTS，一个 30 亿参数的文本转语音模型，以远低于竞争对手的成本达到顶尖水平 —— 并且开源权重。Pavan 透露了其新颖的架构：自回归流匹配(flow matching)方法，配合内部神经音频编解码器，将音频转换为 12.5 赫兹的潜在 token（语义+声学）。这取代了传统的深度变压器(depth transformer)，用更高效的流匹配头显著降低延迟，同时保持质量。

On the broader vision, Guillaume emphasized Mistral's dual strategy: general models plus specialized, highly efficient models for specific use cases. "If you care about this specific use case, you can actually use this model, it just does that. It's extremely good at that, very efficient." He also defended their open-source mission passionately: "We really don't want to be living in a world where the smartest models, the best models are only behind closed doors... We want intelligence to be used and accessible by anyone."

在更宏大的愿景上，Guillaume 强调了 Mistral 的双轨策略：通用模型加上针对特定用例的高度专业化、高效模型。"如果你关心这个特定用例，你可以直接使用这个模型，它只做这个。它极其擅长这个，非常高效。"他还充满激情地捍卫了他们的开源使命："我们真的不想生活在一个最聪明的模型、最好的模型只被关在门后的世界……我们希望任何人都能使用和获取智能。"

The conversation also covered Mistral Small (a 6B active parameter sparse model merging coding, vision, and instruction following), Leanstral (their formal proof model for Lean), and their enterprise Forge platform for private deployment and fine-tuning. On voice agents, Guillaume noted that even in 2026, talking to AI still isn't natural — people still speak slowly and over-articulate. The next frontier is full-duplex conversation where the model can speak while you're speaking.

对话还涵盖了 Mistral Small（60 亿活跃参数的稀疏模型，融合编码、视觉和指令遵循）、Leanstral（用于 Lean 的形式证明模型），以及他们的企业级 Forge 平台（支持私有部署和微调）。在语音 agent 方面，Guillaume 指出即使在 2026 年，与 AI 对话仍然不够自然 —— 人们仍然说得慢且过度清晰。下一个前沿是全双工(full-duplex)对话，模型可以在你说话的同时说话。
