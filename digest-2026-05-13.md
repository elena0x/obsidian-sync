### Box CEO Aaron Levie

Aaron Levie pushed back on the idea that AI agents eliminate work. His argument: when agents do work for you, the work just moves up a layer of abstraction. You become the editor, manager, and producer — figuring out what to tell the agent, giving proper instructions, providing context, intervening when it goes off-task, reviewing output, and incorporating it into something else. The longer the task, the more planning and review stages take up. "Taste matters, understanding the craft matters, and the skills ultimately matter."

Box CEO Aaron Levie 反驳了 AI Agent 会消除工作的观点。他的论点：当 Agent 为你完成工作时，工作只是向上移动了一层抽象。你变成了编辑、管理者和制作人—— figuring out 要告诉 Agent 做什么、给出正确的指令、提供上下文、在 Agent 偏离任务时介入、审阅输出并将其整合到其他东西中。任务越长，规划和审阅阶段所占的时间就越多。"品味很重要，理解 craft 很重要，技能最终也很重要。"

### Latent Space — Mistral: Voxtral TTS, Forge, Leanstral, & what's next for Mistral 4

**The Takeaway:** Mistral is going all-in on multimodal — releasing Voxtral TTS (text-to-speech), Forge (a unified fine-tuning platform), and Leanstral (a formal-math reasoning model) — while teasing that Mistral 4 pretraining will be "a big step up compared to everything we have done before."

**核心要点：** Mistral 正在全力投入多模态——发布 Voxtral TTS（文本转语音）、Forge（统一微调平台）和 Leanstral（形式化数学推理模型）——同时暗示 Mistral 4 的预训练将是"相比我们之前所有工作的巨大飞跃"。

Mistral Chief Scientist Guillaume Lample and Audio Research Lead Pavan Kumar Reddy joined Latent Space to discuss three major releases. Voxtral TTS is their first speech-generation model — a compact 3B parameter model supporting nine languages, designed for real-time voice agents. Unlike competitors, it uses an autoregressive approach built on Mistral's existing transformer decoder backbone, making it a natural extension rather than a separate architecture. The model is open-weight (though not fully open-source this time), and costs a fraction of comparable offerings.

Mistral 首席科学家 Guillaume Lample 和音频研究负责人 Pavan Kumar Reddy 做客 Latent Space，讨论了三个重大发布。Voxtral TTS 是他们的首个语音生成模型——一个紧凑的 30 亿参数模型，支持九种语言，专为实时语音 Agent 设计。与竞争对手不同，它使用基于 Mistral 现有 transformer decoder backbone 的自回归方法，使其成为自然延伸而非独立架构。该模型开放权重（虽然这次不是完全开源），成本仅为同类产品的零头。

Forge is their unified fine-tuning framework — the same infrastructure their internal science team uses for pretraining, SFT, and RL, now available to customers. The pitch: fine-tune on your own data and you can leapfrog closed-source models that don't know your company's knowledge. This is especially relevant for domain-specific use cases — medical terminology, legal jargon, or languages Mistral doesn't natively support yet.

Forge 是他们的统一微调框架——与其内部科学团队用于预训练、SFT 和 RL 的相同基础设施，现在向客户开放。卖点：用自己的数据微调，你可以超越那些不了解你公司知识的闭源模型。这对于特定领域用例尤其 relevant——医学术语、法律 jargon，或 Mistral 尚未原生支持的语言。

Leanstral is the most unexpected release — a formal-math proving model built on Lean. Lample explained that formal proving is valuable because it provides verifiable correctness: "as long as it compiles in Lean, it's correct." This eliminates the reward-hacking problems that plague RL-based reasoning on open-ended problems. But the deeper bet is that long-horizon math proofs are a proxy for general reasoning, coherence, and planning — capabilities that transfer to coding and beyond. The model can even decompose complex theorems into sub-lemmas and prove them in parallel using sub-agents.

Leanstral 是最令人意外的发布——一个基于 Lean 的形式化数学证明模型。Lample 解释说，形式化证明的价值在于它提供了可验证的正确性："只要在 Lean 中编译通过，就是正确的。"这消除了基于 RL 的推理在开放式问题上的 reward hacking 问题。但更深层的赌注是，长程数学证明是通用推理、连贯性和规划的 proxy——这些能力可以转移到 coding 及更广泛的领域。该模型甚至可以将复杂定理分解为子引理，并使用 sub-agent 并行证明。

On what's next: Lample confirmed they're still heavily investing in pretraining, and "Mistral 4 pretraining will be a big step up." On the RL side, they're working on algorithms that support very long trajectories — beyond the few-thousand-token horizon where GRPO currently works. The vision is end-to-end multimodal: text, audio, and vision all in one model, though Lample noted that for transcription specifically, a smaller dedicated model still makes more sense than routing everything through a giant generalist.

关于未来：Lample 确认他们仍在大力投入预训练，"Mistral 4 的预训练将是一个巨大的飞跃。"在 RL 方面，他们正在研究支持非常长轨迹的算法——超越 GRPO 目前适用的几千 token 范围。愿景是端到端多模态：文本、音频和视觉全部集成在一个模型中，尽管 Lample 指出，对于转录 specifically，一个更小的专用模型仍然比将所有内容路由到一个巨大的 generalist 更有意义。
