**Roblox Product Lead Peter Yang** shared five takeaways from his conversation with a16z GP Anish Acharya about the future of work in an AI-agent-first world. Key insights: coding agents will eat all knowledge work (he now gets 80% of docs, slides, and analytics done through AI before manual polish); small 2-3 person teams with agent swarms will outperform bloated orgs; task-completion apps will shrink while entertainment apps endure; and personal agents will understand us deeply — his OpenClaw once reminded him to prioritize time with his young kids over career optimization. He also pushed back on measuring productivity by token usage, calling it "almost as dumb as measuring by lines of code written."
